Injection Mold Design & Validation

Complete injection mold design for an automotive ABS speaker component.

Project Overview

Context: Individual MSc tooling design project focused on the complete design of an injection mold for an automotive speaker component made from ABS.

Problem: The automotive speaker part required a manufacturable mold solution capable of handling parting strategy, extraction, feeding, cooling and complex geometry constraints.

My Role: I designed the complete mold, including mold architecture, parting surfaces, sliders, lifters, ejectors, cooling system, feeding system, CAD assembly and 2D technical drawings.

Duration: Individual MSc project in Mechanical Engineering, specialization in Materials and Technological Processes.

Tools: Siemens NX, CAD modelling, mold design, technical drawing, injection molding principles, design for manufacturing.

Technical Evidence

Recruiter Value

Design for manufacturing: shows understanding of how plastic components become manufacturable parts.

Tooling knowledge: includes sliders, lifters, ejectors, cooling and feeding systems.

CAD maturity: demonstrates complete assembly thinking, not just isolated part modelling.
