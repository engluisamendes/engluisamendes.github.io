VITO Drywall Sander Product Development

Company-collaboration product development project for a redesigned wall and ceiling drywall sander.

Project Overview

Context: Company-collaboration product development project with VITO, developed in an integrated team of mechanical engineering and product design students.

Problem: The project aimed to redesign a wall and ceiling drywall sander by identifying market opportunities, customer needs and technical improvements over the existing product.

My Role: I contributed to CAD development, benchmarking, market research, product architecture and technical definition, including motor and battery selection. The project resulted in a full CAD assembly and a three-tier product proposal targeting different customer needs and price ranges.

Duration: MSc integrated product development project.

Tools: SolidWorks, CAD assembly, benchmarking, market research, product development, motor selection, battery selection, ergonomic design and technical specification.

Technical Evidence

Recruiter Value

Product development: connects market research, user needs, design and technical feasibility.

Mechanical design: demonstrates CAD assembly, ergonomics and component integration.

Industry collaboration: shows ability to work with design students and company constraints.
